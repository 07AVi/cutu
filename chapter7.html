Chapter 6:The boy who still believes

Hey Hanshika,

I hope you liked all this. Honestly, the only reason behind making all this was just to bring a small smile on your face.

And haan, I know it’s your life — you’ll make your own choices. Trust me, I’m not forcing you to do anything.

You know, nowadays whatever I see or do, the first thought that pops in my mind is — “ye Hanshika ke saath karne mein kaisa lagega?” or “is jagah uske saath jaana hota toh kitna accha lagta…”

Main tere saamne kabhi fake nahi tha. Haan, thoda childish tha, stupid tha — but bas kyunki mujhe tujhe woh sab dikhana accha lagta tha. Par haan, I agree, I was too possessive and jealous sometimes.

And I know you probably don’t feel the same way anymore — and that’s okay. Your life, your decisions.

But agar kabhi life ne hume dobara mila diya — I promise this time I’ll be a little less possessive and jealous... aur tu thoda kam gussa kar lena (nahi bhi karegi toh chalega).

Tu hi toh ek baar boli thi na — “being in a relationship, you eventually fall in love.” Agli baar sab kuch tere hisaab se hoga. I just want to grow with you, experience every little thing, travel the world — but with you only.

Aur travel ki sari planning, mehnat, money — sab kuch main handle karunga. I want to be your personal cook , driver , photographer , travel buddy , fuck buddy , therapist , massager, helper, manchild, guide — everything.

I’m not doing all this expecting something back. Mujhe kuch chahiye nahi. Bas karna tha — kyunki tu deserve karti hai har wo cheez jo main kar sakta hoon.

Chahe tera mann ho ya na ho mujhse baat karne ka, I’ll still always respect you... and love you.

Agar future mein milna likha hai — toh milenge. But haan, uske liye mehnat bhi karni padti hai... and that’s what I’m doing now.

Thank you for everything, Hanshika. You mean the world to me.
